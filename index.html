When

Our wedding will take place on the week-end of July 6th to 7th in 2024 in Gothenburg, Sweden.


The formal civil ceremony will be held at the venue on Saturday, the 6th of July.

Dress code: Festive

Master of ceremony and toast master for the day: The One And Only Nils

14:00 Mingle with light snacks

18:00 Dinner

24:00 Midnight snack


On Sunday the 7th of July, we plan a relaxed day with some nature activities with base at the same venue, making use of the forest and lake nearby.

Dress code: Relaxed

12:00 Brunch

14:00 Games

16:00 Canoe on the lake

Where

The wedding location is in the nature reserve Delsjön, in a venue called Kalmaden. The venue is accessible by tram number 5, with a 20 minutes walk. We hope to be outdoors a lot. The building is a typical Swedish wooden construction in rustic style. The page of the venue with exact address and directions.

Practical info


There is no particular dress theme or colour scheme for the wedding. Are you able to dance in your clothes?


The outdoor area is just grass and there is a 20 minute walk on a cleared forest path, so you might want to have flat or sturdy shoes. You can bring a second pair of shoes and change at the venue as well.


You can bring a swimming suit and beach towel for the lake, as well as some comfortable clothes for games, canoeing and walking around the nature reserve.


The weather in Sweden can be unpredictable. You might want to bring clothes for rainy or cold weather.


We do not have a gift list. We are very happy to see you on our wedding day and that is the best gift. If you would like to contribute to our honeymoon, that will be possible.


Our guests are our best entertainers! Do you want to sing, perform a dance, tell a poem? Please do so! Let us know and we'll include you in the plan.


If you have dietary restrictions beyond being vegan/vegetarian, please let us know.



Please refrain from wearing big amounts of perfume or any perfume altogether as Alina's dad has an allergy to it.



Smoking is prohibited in the venue on the grounds. Several guests cannot inhale tobacco smoke for health reasons, please respect that.
