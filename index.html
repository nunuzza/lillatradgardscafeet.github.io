Where

The wedding location is in the nature reserve Delsjön, in a house called Kalmaden. The venue is accessible by tram number 5, with a 20 minutes walk. We hope to be outdoors a lot. The page of the venue.

Hitta oss

Unfortunately it is not possible to sleep inside the venue. For the adventurous, it is possible to camp inside the nature reserve with an own tent. The tram number 5 connects to more residential areas in the east, as well as a more busy city area with regular hotels in the west.

Practical info

The outdoor area is just grass, so you might want to have suitable shoes. The weather in Sweden can be unpredictable. You can bring some swimming suits for the lake, but you might want to bring clothes for rainy weather as well. If you have dietary restrictions beyond being vegan/vegetarian, please let us know.
